Welcome to Zohud

The ZOHUD well known is a non-Government, Non-Commercial, Non-Religious, and No-Political humanitarian organization.e

What is Zohud

The Society is independents from all political, religious and economic interests and seeks to serve the humanity at large. ZOHUD is working for most marginalized and healthy society specially focus on Education, Books Publications, Teachers Training, Social Sector and community Service, Health, Gen Awareness in Society; Social Development and empowerment of deprived masses through at-least providing them with basic education and improves living standard as well as plantation, water management and environment.

  • Bring relief to deprived and marginalized communities who suffer from lack of access to safe Drinking water, basic educational, heal and humanitarian essentials.
  • Positive changes are brought in the unjust social, dignity, economic and cultural attitude for women and other humanity in the background of global warming.
  • Capabilities would be developed of rural community, civil society organization, and various stake holders to work effectively and efficiently to promote environment.
  • Response to global warming in natural and man-made calamities and disasters.
  • To work for the climate change uplift of the marginalized communities of Pakistan with special focus on the improvement in education, health, livelihoods through participatory community development process.

OUR MISSION

We believe in human development and dignity especially women development and to inspire social action for attitudinal change in which everyone feels responsible and accountable and promote values that build up a healthy atmosphere for progress.

OUR VISION

As society where human especially Women values reign supreme and women live comfortably.

AIMS AND OBJECTS

ZOHUD has wide range to improve and develop the society with its Motto, “LIFE AND KNOWLEDGE”, and Life for healthy Society qualifies educated society to enhance other planning to repudiate the curse of society. To realize and individuals, groups, institutions and media to ensure their financial & moral support for the improvement of Education, Health, Environment, Human Development and Human Rights, Technical Promotion, vocational and Health sector, AND Capacity Building, Resources, and further aims and objectives for which the Association is going to establish are given as under: -

  • EDUCATION
  • BOOKS PUBLICATIONS
  • TEACHERS TRAINING
  • SOCIAL SECTOR AND COMMUNITY SERVICE
  • HEALTH
  • GENERAL AWARENESS IN SOCIETY
  • To promote Education, Health and sustainable development, to work for primary health of the needy and poor peoples to establish and run Hospitals, Medical Centers, specially to establish, manage & run Hospitals, with its branches and other Dispensaries, with Labs Operation Theater & Labs.
  • To provide free medical facilities to deserving people by mean of providing / supply of food, nourishment items, clothing, medicines and medical aid and free medical tests facilities etc.
  • To establish and run institutions, schools, colleges, universities, of General Education and Medical College(s) with branch(es), Elementary Schools, Technical Centers, Computer Science, Empowerment Centers, vocational centers, to organize the units in different areas, research on disease of human, drug abuse, to work against corruption or criminal activities, child labour, and to educate the people for the human rights.
  • To establish and arrange borings and wells for drinking and other utilization of water, to work for development and basic needs necessities of life of the communities, to decrease the poverty from the communities with enhancement of ability, awareness, modern education, earning planning and health care.
  • To promote vocational training and skills development for women, and to arrange and create benefits for them at their residences, and to hate the low & high differences in between woman & man to create fund system, and financial support, micro financing without markup and to provide funds, finance to the disable or needy peoples, specially to arrange & provide marriage expenditures of poor girls, to provide funeral expenditures for poor & heirless dead bodies etc.
  • To organize, support and facilitate an those efforts through various sources of mass media including electronic, print media and social media required for awareness and capacity building of the members and to aware and make connection in between the public and Government and Non Government and Private Sectors.
  • To arrange / conduct exhibitions, Seminars, conferences workshop training programs; to Celebrate National, Provincial and cultural days, for the welfare and awareness of the people.
  • To collaborate cooperate with other institutions, NGO’S Associations, trusts having similar activities of charity & public benefits and to create coordination and linkage with other Associations, Donors and other line department for sustainable development and to make it comprehensive and beneficial for community.
  • To secure, receive, accept and manage funds, donations, grants, endowments and any other moveable or immovable properties from lawful sources within or outside country and to utilize them for the furthering and promoting the aims and objects of the Association, subject to the approval of competent authority.
  • The Association shall confine its activities only to the objects of the Association as set fourth in the Memorandum of Association and income and property of the association from whatever source derived shall be applied solely towards the promoting the aims and objects of the Association and no portion whereof shall be paid by whereof dividend profit, bonus to any member, office bearer of the association or otherwise, violation of this condition shall the personal responsibility of the office bearer and the member concerned of the Association.
  • The Association shall be run on non-political, non commercial and non-religious basis, and there is no dispute amongst the members.
  • The promoters / subscribers of the Association shall not be paid employees by any manner.

RULES & REGULATIONS / ARTICLES OF THE ASSOCIATION

NAMELY ZOHUD
NAME OF SOCIETY:
The name of the Association shall be as: -
“ZOHUD”hereinafter called the Association.
ADDRESS OF THE SOCIETY:
The registered office of the said Society shall be at: -
Head Office to be launched soon

  • AREA OF THE OPERATION. The scope of the Association’s activities shall be in community of all Sindh Province and then may all over with benefit from the work and experiences of the Association.
  • NATURE OF THE ASSOCIATION. The Association shall be run on Non-Sectarian, Non-Commercial, Non-Political and Non Religious nature.
  • MEMBERSHIP. The membership of the Association shall be opened to all those adults, possessing sound mind good moral character, who are interested and willing to work for propagate and uplift the association and other benevolent etc, and Promises to abide the Rules, Regulations, /Articles of the Association and subject to the approval of the Managing committee if he/she: -
    a. Promise to abide by the all Rules and Regulations of the Association.
    b. Pay admission fee of Rs.300/- (Rupees Three Hundred only) and annual subscription fee of Rs.800/- in equal Four monthly installments.
    c. If approved and considered by the Managing Committee.
    d. The Executive Committee shall have the right to admit any person as an honorary members if may deem fit.
  • GENERAL MANAGING COMMITTEE. Only all members of the Association from the General Body, the Management of the Association shall be called the EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE / MANAGING COMMITTEE. The General Body shall elect Office bearers such as: -
    a. PRESIDENT.
    b. VICE PRESIDENT.
    c. GENERAL SECRETARY.
    d. JOINT SECRETARY.
    e. FINANCE SECRETARY.
    f. FINANCE SECRETRY (TREASURER).
    g. MEMBERS.
    5. FUNCTIONS OF THE OFFICE BEARERS
    a. PRESIDENT
    (1) He/ She will supervise, look after manage the all affairs and will be responsible for the entire working and Management of the Association and its finances.
    (2) He/ She will preside over all the meetings / Executive Committee of the Association.
    (3) He/ She will, however authorized to any or all of his powers to the vice President.
    (4) He/ She will have the power of casting vote in case of equality of vote.
    (5) Conduct all correspondence on behalf of the Association.
    (6) Conduct all working, maintain the office and carryout all executive work of the Association.
    (7) Perform all other allied and necessary duties for day to day Administration of the Association.
    b. VICE PRESIDENT. He/ she will preside any meeting in the absence of the President or when authorized by the President shall officiate for her during her absence and will exercise the same practice of the President.
    c. GENERAL SECRETARY.
    (1) He/ She will maintain a Register of Members.
    (2) Keep record of all meetings and proceedings of the Executive Committee and the Association.
    (3) Call meetings of the Executive Committee and the Association, in consultation with the President.
    (4) Circulate notice and Agenda of all meetings to the members.
    (5) Prepare the Annual report.
    (6) Keep with him/ her a sum of Rs.1000/- as imp reset for incidental miscellaneous expenditure of the Association.
    d. TREASURER/ FINANCE SECRETARY. He/ She will maintain the Register of Accounts of the income and expenditures of the Association. He will prepare the Annual report of Audit, Balance Sheet.
    e. JOINT SECRETARY. He/ She will assist the General Secretary and in the absence of General Secretary all his/ her duties will be perform by the Joint Secretary or any other duties will be given to him/ her by the General Secretary.
    f. MEMBERS EXECUTIVE/MANAGING COMMITTEE (CENTRAL). The Members Executive / Managing Committee will provide the suggestions and advised about the matters and decisions of the Foundation, which may be accepted by the Managing / Executive Committee and / or may be, approved in General Body meetings.
  • ELECTIONS
    a. The election for office bearers and members of Managing committee may be held after very (3) Three years within two months of the expiry of the term of financial year within two months if suspended.
    b. The General Body will elect the same or new Managing Committee members/ office bearers in the General Body meeting held at a special date with such notice being put up on the Notice Board in the premised office of the Association.
    8. BANK ACCOUNTS. The Bank Account of the Association shall be operated jointly by the PRESIDENT, GENERAL SECRETARY& TREASURER (FINANCE SECRETARY) their signatures and the cheque may be got encashed with the only two signatures but including the TREASURER (FINANCE SECRETRY).
  • FUNCTION OF EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE. The Functions of the Executive Committee shall be: -
    a. To collect and control the entire Management of the Association.
    b. In fraise such rules as may be necessary for the efficient working of the Association.
    c. To fill up any casual vacancy in the Executive Committee.
  • OFFICIAL YEAR. The official year of the Association shall be just according to the financial year.
  • CASTING OF VOTES. All the decisions of the Association shall be made by majority of votes. The votes shall be caste by secret ballet members in papers shall not be entitled to caste votes.
  • ANNUAL GENERAL MEETING OF THE ASSOCIATION: -
    a. The annual General meeting of the Association shall be held once a year in the Court of June, every calendar month.
    b. In consider and pass the statements of Accounts for the previous year.
    c. Whenever due to eject the Official bearers of the Society.
    d. In consider the report of the General Secretary.
    e. To discuss any matter that may be noticed by the Chairman or any Member of Association.
  • QUORUM FOR THE GENERAL MEETING: -
    a. The quorum for the General Body meeting shall be 2/3rd of the total numbers of members of the roll.
    b. The Executive Committee shall be authorized to call an extra ordinalry meeting of the Association for any purpose on a written requisition duly signed by not less then 2/3rd members of on the roll.
  • NOTICE OF ANNUAL GENERAL BODY MEETING OF BODY. Fifteen days notice specifying the time, date of the General Body meeting shall be given with the Agenda and a copy of the statement of Accounts for the year.
  • PROCEEDURE OF THE MEETING. The President shall preside all the meetings of the Central Executive Committee and the Association. In the meeting any matter duly proposed by the President for the meeting and exercise or caste vote in case of equality of votes, all matters shall be decided in a meeting by majority of votes or show of hands.
  • EXPELSION OF OFFICE BEARERS AND MEMBER: -
    a. Non payment of subscription fee upto 30 days after due date, the General Secretary will issue notice to all members who are in arrears prior to the due date. A second notice of 30 days will be given at the expiry of first notice period. If the dues are not cleared as per second notice, the persons concerned will be expelled.
    b. If the activity of any member is considered by the Executive Committee to be inimical and anti to the aims and objects of the Association, the General Body may expel such member if decided by 2/3rd majority of the members present in the meeting specially called for. Full opportunity would be given to such member or office bearer for the defence before the General Body in person or in written.
    c. If a member fails to attend three consecutive meetings without prior information, with justification his membership may be suspended as well as cancelled by the decision of the General Body.
  • RESIGNATION. Any member may of his own accord, may terminate his membership by sending her resignation in writing to the General Secretary or President.
  • RESTORATION OF MEMBERSHIP. The Managing Committee restore membership of any person on written request for the person with approval of Managing Committee by majority of votes, whose membership has been suspended only not cancelled.
  • LIABILITIES AND ASSETS OF ASSOCIATION. All liabilities and assets of the Association shall vest in the Association and shall not be deemed to be the property of any member or office bearer of the Association and for the benefits of the Association, the Managing Committee of the Association just by majority of 2/3rd will be fully entitled to purchase or th sell out any property or assets of the Association. All assets, properties of the Organization may be sold out or may be purchased for Organization by joint signatures of President and General Secretary.
  • AUDIT. All the accounts, check and balance of the Association shall be audited from the concerned charted accountant every year regularly AND annual report will be deposited in the office of Assistant Joint Stock Companies, Hyderabad.
  • AMENDMENT IN CONSTITUTION. Any amendments, Insertion, addision and alteration to the articles and Memorandum of the Association shall be made by a ample majority of votes in an extra ordinary meeting of the General Body of the Association as and when required or directed by the Government.
  • WINDING UP: -
    a. The Association may be dissolved in accordance with Rules and Regulations of the Societies Act 1860 with its Section.
    b. If 2/3rd members of the Association decide in a General Body meeting called for the purpose of dissolve the Association they may do so, on winding up the Association and the assets of the Association shall be transferred to any other association established under the Societies Registration Act 1860 having similar aims and objects and the funds and properties of the Association will not be distributed amongst the members.
